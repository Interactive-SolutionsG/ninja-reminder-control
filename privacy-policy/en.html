Privacy Policy - Ninja Reminder Control

Last Updated: May 2026

Version: 1.0

1. General Information

Ninja Reminder Control is a calendar and reminders application built with the utmost respect for your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect (or rather, what we DO NOT collect) and how we protect your privacy.

Developer: Interactive Solutions

Contact: interactive.sup.solutions@outlook.com

2. Privacy Commitment

Core Principle:

Ninja Reminder Control DOES NOT collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.

3. Data We DO NOT Collect

This application DOES NOT collect any of the following data:

Personal Data:

  • Name, address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Geographic location
  • IP address

Usage Data:

  • Application usage statistics
  • Telemetry or behavioral analytics
  • Usage hours
  • Usage frequency

Technical Data:

  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Hardware specifications
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Error logs or diagnostics

Note on device identifiers:

Microsoft Store uses device identifiers to manage licenses across your devices. However, we as developers DO NOT have access to these identifiers. Microsoft Store only provides us with a "valid license" or "invalid license" response without revealing device information.

Reminder and Calendar Data:

  • Your scheduled reminders
  • Calendar notes
  • Times and dates
  • Titles and descriptions

EVERYTHING stays on your local device.

4. Data Storage

Local Data Only

All information you configure in Ninja Reminder Control is stored exclusively on your local device in:

%LOCALAPPDATA%\NinjaReminderControl\

Files created locally:

  • Reminders and calendar notes
  • Recurring events
  • User preferences
  • Selected language
  • Notification style

These files:

  • Only exist on your computer
  • Are NOT synced to any server
  • Are NOT accessible to the developer
  • Can be deleted at any time

5. Communication with External Servers

No Internet Connection Required

Ninja Reminder Control DOES NOT require an internet connection to function and DOES NOT communicate with any external server.

Exceptions:

1. Microsoft Store:

  • When you download or update the application from Microsoft Store
  • To validate your license or trial period
  • Managed by Microsoft

2. Updates (Optional):

  • Only if you manually check for updates
  • NO personal information is sent, only the current app version

THERE IS NO:

  • Third-party analytics
  • Advertising services
  • Trackers
  • Cookies

If you decide to purchase Ninja Reminder Control:

  • Microsoft Store processes all payments
  • Microsoft Store stores your payment information
  • Microsoft Store identifies your device and Microsoft Account
  • We DO NOT have access to:
  • Your payment information
  • Your payment method
  • Banking or card details
  • Purchase history
  • Unique identifiers of your device
  • Your Microsoft Account

We only know:

  • Whether your license is active
  • Whether you are in the trial period
  • Trial start date

For more information:

See the Microsoft Store Privacy Policy

7. Application Permissions

Minimal Permissions

Ninja Reminder Control requests only the permissions needed to operate:

Access to local file system

  • For: Saving reminders, notes and preferences
  • Required: Yes

Notifications

  • For: Displaying scheduled reminders
  • Required: Yes — it's the core function of the app

Windows Task Scheduler

  • For: Firing reminders even when the app is closed
  • Required: Yes

Run at Windows startup

  • For: Recovering missed reminders after PC boot
  • Required: No, but recommended

Internet client (internetClient)

  • For: Validating the license with Microsoft Store
  • Required: Yes, only for Microsoft Store

WE DO NOT request:

  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts access
  • Location access
  • Photo/video access

8. Use of Information

Since we DO NOT collect any data, there is NO use of personal information.

The only data that exists is:

  • Local configuration on your device
  • License state (managed by Microsoft Store)

9. Sharing Information with Third Parties

WE DO NOT Share Data

There is no data collection.

We do not share, sell or rent:

  • Personal data
  • Usage data
  • Reminder information
  • Any information at all

10. Data Security

Local Protection

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • You are responsible for the physical security of your computer
  • Windows manages file access permissions
  • We recommend:
  • Use a Windows account password
  • Keep Windows updated
  • Use a trusted antivirus

On our side:

  • We DO NOT store data on servers
  • There is NO risk of data leakage from us
  • There are NO security breaches against servers

11. User Rights

Your Rights

Since we DO NOT collect personal data, most privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) do not apply, but we respect your rights:

  • Access to your data: All of it is on your device at %LOCALAPPDATA%\NinjaReminderControl\
  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app or delete the folder manually
  • Portability: Copy configuration files to another device
  • Opposition: Not applicable (no collection)
  • Rectification: Modify reminders directly through the app interface
